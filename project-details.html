How we redesigned the hotel booking platform to provide better information and increase conversions

OVERVIEW

This project focused on improving the hotel booking experience by addressing critical content gaps that were causing user drop-offs. Our approach combined comprehensive content strategy with intuitive UI design to provide users with the information they needed at each step of their journey.

Client

Travel Platform Inc.

Role

 Product DesignerContent Strategist

Timeline

12 weeks

THE PROBLEM

Hotels were struggling with inconsistent content across their listings. Users were dropping off during the booking process due to insufficient information about amenities, room features, and policies. This led to decreased trust and lower conversion rates.

"I often abandon bookings because I can't find information about cancellation policies or whether the room has the specific amenities I need."

OBJECTIVE

Our goal was to standardize hotel content across the platform, ensuring customers have all the information they need to make informed booking decisions. By addressing these content gaps, we aimed to:

  • Increase booking conversion rates by 15%
  • Reduce customer support inquiries by 30%
  • Improve overall customer satisfaction scores

DESIGN PROCESS

1. Research & Discovery

We analyzed user feedback, support tickets, and drop-off points in the booking funnel to identify common information gaps.

2. Content Standardization

We developed a comprehensive content framework that standardized information across all hotel listings.

3. UI Design Solutions

Our design approach focused on surfacing the most critical information earlier in the user journey.

4. Testing & Validation

We conducted multiple rounds of usability testing to validate our solutions, making iterative improvements based on user feedback.

OUTCOME

The redesigned hotel content experience resulted in significant improvements across key metrics:

  • 18% increase in booking conversion rates, exceeding our goal
  • 42% reduction in customer support inquiries about hotel amenities and policies
  • Customer satisfaction scores improved from 3.6/5 to 4.5/5
"The new detail pages give me everything I need to know before booking. I feel much more confident in my choices now."

GALLERY

KEY LEARNINGS

This project reinforced the importance of comprehensive content strategy in e-commerce experiences. By addressing information gaps, we not only improved conversion metrics but also enhanced customer trust and satisfaction.

Moving forward, we've implemented a content governance system to ensure all new hotel listings meet our content standards before going live on the platform.
