Background Overview

Syazwan is a Senior Consultant in Ernst & Young Technology Consulting in the Digital & Emerging Technology practice, focusing in Front-end User Interface Design Development.

Syazwan joined Ernst & Young in 2019 and is based in the Menara Millenium, Kuala Lumpur office.

Syazwan graduated in Diploma of Graphic Design, majoring in Advertising & Marketing in 2011. Syazwan has seven years of working experience in Digital Design and UI/UX.
