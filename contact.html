Factors Influencing Your Project Cost

While my packages provide clear starting points, the final cost of your project can be influenced by a few key factors:

Number of Pages & Complexity

More pages and intricate layouts naturally require more development time.

Custom Features

Specific functionalities like e-commerce, payment gateway integration, multi-language support, or advanced integrations will impact the price.

Content Creation

Whether you provide all the content (copywriting, images) or need my assistance will affect the overall cost.

Ongoing Services

Annual costs for domain, hosting, maintenance, and advanced SEO are separate from the one-time project fee.

Unlock Funding for Your Digital Transformation!

It's worth noting that Malaysian Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) can often apply for government grants to support their digitalization efforts. The MSME Digital Grant, an initiative by MDEC, specifically covers services like web design and e-commerce development.

I can guide you on how to explore and potentially leverage these grants to offset the cost of your web design project. This can make a professional online presence even more accessible for your business.
